About This Property

A rare opportunity in the heart of Toronto

This beautifully renovated detached home offers an exceptional blend of modern luxury and timeless character. Nestled on a quiet, tree-lined street in one of Toronto's most sought-after neighbourhoods, the property features soaring ceilings, wide-plank hardwood floors throughout, and a sun-drenched open-concept main floor perfect for entertaining. The chef's kitchen has been completely reimagined with custom cabinetry, waterfall quartz countertops, and top-of-the-line appliances. The primary suite is a true retreat — spacious enough for a seating area, with a spa-inspired ensuite and walk-in closet. The backyard features a professionally landscaped garden and a private deck ideal for summer evenings.
